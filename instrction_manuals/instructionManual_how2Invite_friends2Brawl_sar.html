Instruction Manual: How to Invite Friends to Your Club in Brawl Stars

Objective:

To guide players through the steps required to invite friends from their friends list to join their club in Brawl Stars.

Requirements:

  • A club that you are a part of in Brawl Stars.
  • Friends added to your friends list.
  • Your friend is not currently in another club or meets the club's trophy requirements.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Open Brawl Stars

1. Launch Brawl Stars on your device.

  • Make sure you are connected to the internet.
  • Log in with your account if needed.

Step 2: Navigate to the Social Menu

1. Once on the main screen of Brawl Stars, look for the Social icon.

  • This is the button with three figures located on the right-hand side of your screen.

Step 3: Access the Club Tab

1. Tap the Club tab to access the club that you are currently in.

  • This tab is also located within the Social menu, typically labeled as Clubs.

Step 4: Open Your Friends List

1. In the Social menu, tap the Friends tab at the bottom of the screen.

  • This will display all the friends you have added in Brawl Stars.
  • Friends will be listed as either Online or Offline.

Step 5: Invite Friends to the Club

1. Scroll through your friends list and select the friend you wish to invite.

  • Tap on your friend's profile picture.
  • This will bring up their player profile.

2. In their profile, there will be an option labeled Invite to Club.

  • Tap this option to send the invitation.

Note:

  • If the Invite to Club option is not available, it could mean:
    • Your friend is already in another club.
    • Your club's trophy requirements are higher than your friend's current trophies.

Step 6: Adjust Club Trophy Requirements (Optional)

1. If your friend doesn’t meet the trophy requirements:

  • You can lower the trophy requirement temporarily to allow them to join.
  • Go to your Club page, tap on the Settings icon, and adjust the required trophy count.

Step 7: Wait for Acceptance

1. Your friend will receive the invitation and can accept or decline it.

2. If they accept, they will join your club and will be visible on the club member list.

Alternative: Invite from the Club Page

1. You can also invite members directly from your Club page:

  • Go to the Club page and tap Invite Members.
  • Search for your friend by their player tag or name and send them an invite directly.

Troubleshooting:

  • Invite button missing: Ensure your friend is not currently in another club.
  • Trophy Requirement: Adjust the club’s trophy requirement if your friend doesn’t meet the threshold.
  • Club full: If the club has reached its member limit, free up a slot or choose a different friend.

FAQs:

Q1. What if my friend is in another club?

They will need to leave their current club before you can send them an invite.

Q2. How can I add more friends in Brawl Stars?

You can add friends by going to the Friends tab and selecting Add Friend. You’ll need their player tag or social media connections.

Q3. Can I invite multiple friends at once?

No, you’ll need to send invitations individually to each friend.

When to Use This Manual:

Use this manual whenever you wish to invite friends to join your club, ensure all requirements are met, and follow these steps to send invitations smoothly.
