The Fourth Industrial Revolution

The fourth industrial revolution has brought about significant changes in the ways businesses work, manufacture, provide services, and interact with their consumers. A key trend is the development of technology-enabled platforms that combine both demand and supply to disrupt existing industry structures, such as those we see within the “sharing” or “on-demand” economy.

Entry barriers for businesses and individuals have been lowered, causing fast-changing impacts for employees and working professionals.
