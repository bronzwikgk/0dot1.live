ITIL ® Master – Single Source of Truth

Author: Sunil Mohal, ITIL ® 4 Master, Accredited Trainer

The ITIL Master Designation is a highly regarded certification for IT Service Management Professionals, awarded to practitioners demonstrating a broad range of knowledge across Service Management Guidance.

The latest approach to achieve this designation from Peoplecert involves holding the following three designations:

  • ITIL ® Strategic Leader
  • ITIL ® MP / MPT
  • ITIL ® Practice Manager
What is the Current Structure of the Certification Schema?

Let us first look at the ITIL 4 Certification scheme, from which various designations, including the ITIL 4 Master Designation, are derived.

The ITIL 4 Certification Structure

The current structure of the ITIL 4 Certification is based on:

  • The ITIL 4 Foundation certification as a prerequisite for all other ITIL Certifications (except for two extension modules).
  • Three main designations professionals can aspire to:
    • ITIL 4 Practice Manager
    • ITIL 4 Managing Professional
    • ITIL 4 Strategic Leader
  • Achieving all three designations qualifies a professional for the ITIL 4 Master designation.
The Practice Manager Designation

This designation demonstrates a professional’s practical and technical knowledge on working within, operating, and implementing successful IT and digital services.

Requirements for this designation include completing:

  • One of three ITIL 4 Specialist Modules: MSF (Monitor Support Fulfill), PIC (Plan Implement Control), or CAI (Collaborate Assure Improve)
  • Or any five of the fifteen Individual Practitioner Modules.
The Managing Professional Designation

The Managing Professional (MP) stream provides practical and technical knowledge on running successful IT-enabled services and workflows. The required modules are:

  • ITIL 4 Specialist: Create, Deliver and Support
  • ITIL 4 Specialist: Drive Stakeholder Value
  • ITIL 4 Specialist: High-velocity IT
  • ITIL 4 Strategist: Direct, Plan and Improve
The Strategic Leader Designation

The ITIL Strategic Leader Designation recognizes the value of ITIL across digitally-enabled services, demonstrating an understanding of how IT drives business strategy. Modules required are:

  • ITIL 4 Strategist: Direct, Plan and Improve
  • ITIL 4 Leader: Digital and IT Strategy

Achieving all three designations automatically grants the prestigious “ITIL ® 4 Master” designation.
