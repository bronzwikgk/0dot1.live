Course Overview

The ITIL 4 Relationship Management Practice Module is structured and aligned with the ITIL framework.

It assesses candidates' understanding of relationship management concepts, including processes, roles, value streams, and capability assessment.

Target Group

This certification is best suited for professionals in Service Delivery, including IT Managers, Support Teams, System Administrators, Operations Managers, Database Administrators, and Service Delivery Professionals.

Concepts Covered

Holders of the ITIL ® 4 PM : Relationship Management, will be able to demonstrate their knowledge and understanding of:

  • Key concepts and principles
  • Processes of the practice
  • Roles and competences required
  • Information and technology enablement
  • Role of partners and suppliers
  • Using the ITIL capability model to develop the practice
  • Recommendations for success in Relationship Management

Exam Format

ITIL ® 4 PM : Relationship Management, exam format is following:

  • Multiple choice format
  • 20 questions per paper
  • 65% to pass, or 13/20
  • 30 minutes’ duration
  • Closed book

Prerequisites -

  • ITIL ® 4 Foundation
