Are you an industry expert who specializes in a particular field such as digital marketing, project management, IT Service Management – ITIL, Devops, Do you have what it takes to create eloquent, data-driven thought-leadership content that engages working professionals in the field?

Come blog for us and see why Ducisgroup is the go-to resource for today’s professionals in the digital economy. Below you’ll find our guidelines to ensure your submission meets our style and topical requirements, and information about our approval process.

Guidelines

Our readers come to the Ducisgroup platform to gain valuable insights into the latest industry trends, innovative business strategies and transformative technologies. The articles published on our site are read by a diverse global audience, including industry leaders, entrepreneurs and professionals at all stages of their careers.

By writing for Ducisgroup, you have the opportunity to build a reputation as a thought leader in one or more of the many forward-thinking topics we cover. Your work will be featured alongside contributions from some of the most influential voices in the business world, ensuring that you’re in excellent company.

When your article is published, it will be accompanied by your own featured author page, complete with your headshot, biography and a link to your personal blog or website. This author page serves as a personal archive of your content and a showcase of your expertise to our global readership.

Contributing to Ducisgroup also opens up opportunities for compensation for your submissions, rewarding your valuable insights and contributions. Join us in shaping the conversations that drive industries forward and leave your mark on a platform that values innovation, quality and impact.

We provide training and certification to more than a million learners worldwide with over 400 in-depth courses. Our content supports those courses by educating learners of all levels what’s new and important in the following categories:

  • Agile and Scrum
  • Project Management
  • IT Service Management - ITIL
  • SDI - Service Desk Manager
  • SIAM
  • ISO Training
  • Devops Insitute - DOI

Our blog contributions take several forms, including the following:

    Industry Insights: Articles offering in-depth analysis and commentary on current trends, challenges and innovations in various industries. These pieces are informed by research and expert opinions, providing valuable perspectives and actionable knowledge to help readers navigate market dynamics.

    Success Stories: Detailed case studies showcasing how businesses or individuals have achieved notable successes with Ducisgroup’s services. These stories highlight challenges faced, solutions implemented and results achieved, demonstrating the tangible impact of Ducisgroup’s offerings.

    Practical Guides: Step-by-step tutorials designed to help professionals apply techniques and best practices in their work. These guides include technical explanations, visual aids and examples to simplify complex concepts and enhance practical skills.

    Market Trends: Articles exploring the latest developments and emerging patterns within industries. They analyze how trends might affect businesses and professionals, offering insights on adapting to and capitalizing on these changes.

    Opinion Pieces: Posts presenting Ducisgroup’s viewpoints on significant industry issues, technologies, or business strategies. These articles offer thought-provoking perspectives and recommendations, incorporating expert analyses and predictions.

    Research Reports: Comprehensive findings from surveys, studies and data analyses conducted by Ducisgroup. These reports provide detailed insights into industry trends, consumer behavior and other relevant topics to guide business decisions.

    Event Recaps: Summaries and analyses of key takeaways from industry events, conferences or seminars attended or hosted by Ducisgroup. These recaps highlight important discussions and their relevance to the audience.

    Infographics: Visually engaging graphics that present complex data and statistics in an easy-to-understand format. Infographics are designed to convey a clear and compelling story about various topics relevant to Ducisgroup’s audience.

    Interviews and Q&A: Features conversations with industry experts, thought leaders, or clients, offering unique insights and personal experiences. These posts provide diverse perspectives on relevant topics and valuable advice.

    Product Spotlights: Articles highlighting the features, benefits, and success stories related to Ducisgroup’s products or services. These spotlights provide an overview of each offering and how it can benefit potential clients.

Draft Submission: Submit your article draft between 800 and 1,000 words in MS Word format (.docx/.doc). Ensure your content is well-researched, up-to-date and provides valuable insights for our readers.

Author Biography: Include a brief personal description of your professional qualifications. Provide links to your portfolios, websites or LinkedIn profiles. This biography will be featured alongside your article upon publication.

Headshot: Attach a headshot of yourself with a minimum resolution of 300 x 300 pixels. This image will be used on the article page and your author page.

Content Quality: Make sure your article has an engaging introduction and conclusion, clearly identifying the target audience and the benefits of the content.

Supporting Materials: While photos are optional, you may include charts or graphs to support your article. Ensure they are original or if not, provide links to sources for obtaining permission or finding suitable alternatives. Images should be in .jpeg, .gif or .png format, with a minimum resolution of 600 x 350 pixels. Attach images separately from the Word document and include concise alternate texts and captions.

Submission: Send your article, bio and images to info@ducisgroup.com We aim to respond within 7 days. If your article is not selected for publication, you retain the right to publish it elsewhere.
